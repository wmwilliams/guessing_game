Wyatt's Guessing Game

This is my very basic game that I wrote in Javascript, its not too exciting or difficult, interested?

Do you...

  • Have some free time?
  • Like stupid games?
  • Want to guess my age?
  • Do you want to have as much fun as my dogs dressed as dinosaurs?

Directions

  1. Its super simple to play!
  2. All you have to do is guess a number.
  3. If you get it right, then good job you win!
  4. If you get it wrong, be careful! You only get one more guess.
  5. Answer incorrectly twice, and its game over! Hope you enjoyed playing
